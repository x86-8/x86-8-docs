DAS - Decimal Adjust for Subtraction





	Usage:	DAS
	Modifies flags: AF CF PF SF ZF (OF undefined)


	Corrects result (in AL) of a previous BCD subtraction operation.
	Contents of AL are changed to a pair of packed decimal digits.
