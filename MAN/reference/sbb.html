SBB - Subtract with Borrow




	Usage:	SBB	dest,src
	Modifies flags: AF CF OF PF SF ZF


	Subtracts the source from the destination, and subtracts 1 extra if
	the Carry Flag is set.	 Results are returned in "dest".
