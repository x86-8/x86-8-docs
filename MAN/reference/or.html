OR - Inclusive Logical OR




	Usage:	OR	dest,src
	Modifies flags: CF OF PF SF ZF (AF undefined)

	Logical inclusive OR of the two operands returning the result in
	the destination.  Any bit set in either operand will be set in the
	destination.
