INC - Increment





	Usage:	INC	dest
	Modifies flags: AF OF PF SF ZF


	Adds one to destination unsigned binary operand.


				 Clocks			Size
	Operands	 808x  286   386   486		Bytes

	reg8		  3	2     2	    1		  2
	reg16		  3	2     2	    1		  1
	reg32		  3	2     2	    1		  1
	mem		15+EA	7     6	    3		 2-4  (W88=23+EA)
